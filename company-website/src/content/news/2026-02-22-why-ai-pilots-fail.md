## Common failure pattern

Teams start with tools, not workflows. They add AI to existing friction instead of redesigning the handoff.

## Better sequence

1. Define one operational bottleneck.
2. Add AI only where cycle-time is blocked.
3. Keep a human approval gate until quality stabilises.
4. Expand scope only after hard KPI improvement.

## Bottom line

AI is not the project. Throughput and reliability are the project.
