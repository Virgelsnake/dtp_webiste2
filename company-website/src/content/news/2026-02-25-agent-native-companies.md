AI adoption is accelerating — but most organisations are deploying it backwards.

They are enhancing workflows.

They are not redesigning them.

At DTP, we are increasingly seeing a different model emerge: **agent-native companies** — businesses architected with AI agents as core execution layers.

This is not automation at the margins.

It is structural redesign.
