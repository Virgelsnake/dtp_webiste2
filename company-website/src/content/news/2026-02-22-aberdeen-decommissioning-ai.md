## Why this matters now

Engineering teams are spending too much time writing and formatting documentation instead of solving real operational problems.

## The practical shift

- Standardise recurring sections once
- Auto-populate evidence tables from structured inputs
- Keep human sign-off for final compliance submission

## What to do this quarter

1. Pick one recurring report type.
2. Create a reusable template and quality checklist.
3. Measure cycle time before/after for 4 weeks.

This is where AI is strongest: reducing repetitive effort while preserving accountable human review.
