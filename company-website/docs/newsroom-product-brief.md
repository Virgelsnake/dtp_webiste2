# Product Brief — DTP Automated Newsroom Service

## Objective
Create a reliable AI-assisted, human-approved newsroom workflow for DTP that removes content production pain for both:
1. News tile summaries (listing cards)
2. Full expanded news articles

## Problem
DTP needs consistent, high-quality website news output without manual writing bottlenecks.

## Desired Outcome
- Repeatable content pipeline from topic discovery to approved publish
- Faster throughput with clear editorial governance
- Reusable delivery model that can be packaged by AI With Agency for other clients

## Users
- Primary: DTP leadership/marketing owner
- Secondary: AI With Agency delivery team

## Scope (v1)
- Topic shortlist generation
- Draft article generation
- Human review and approval gate
- Publish-ready markdown content
- News listing and article detail pages

## Out of Scope (v1)
- Full CMS migration
- Fully autonomous publishing without human approval

## Success Metrics
- Weekly approved article output consistency
- Reduced time from idea to published article
- No unapproved content published
- Reuse-ready process and templates for external clients

## Constraints
- Human approval mandatory before publish
- Source transparency and disclosure retained
- Keep implementation practical and low-maintenance
